# Source: Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 — Application dates (Article 113)

> Reference excerpt for citation. Condensed for retrieval. Verify against the consolidated Official
> Journal text before relying on exact wording.

The Regulation entered into force on **1 August 2024** (twentieth day after publication in the OJ)
and applies from **2 August 2026**, with the following exceptions:

- **Chapter I** (general provisions, incl. Article 4 AI literacy) and **Chapter II** (Article 5
  prohibited practices) apply from **2 February 2025**.
- **Chapter III Section 4** (notifying authorities/notified bodies), **Chapter V** (general-purpose
  AI models), **Chapter VII** (governance), **Chapter XII** (penalties) and **Article 78**
  (confidentiality) apply from **2 August 2025** — except Article 101 (fines for GPAI providers).
- **Article 6(1)** and the corresponding obligations for high-risk AI systems that are safety
  components of products covered by **Annex I** Union harmonisation legislation apply from
  **2 August 2027**.

Key takeaways for an SME:
- AI literacy and prohibited-practice bans are **already in force** (since 2 Feb 2025).
- Annex III high-risk obligations and Article 50 transparency obligations apply from
  **2 August 2026** — the primary planning deadline.
- Product-safety (Annex I) high-risk obligations have the longer runway to **2 August 2027**.
