# EU AI Act — Application timeline

Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 entered into force **1 August 2024** and applies in staggered phases.
Use these dates to tell the company what is already overdue vs. imminent.

| Date | What applies |
|------|--------------|
| **1 Aug 2024** | Act enters into force. |
| **2 Feb 2025** | **Prohibited practices** (Article 5) apply. **AI literacy** (Article 4) applies. *(Already in force — overdue for non-compliant SMEs.)* |
| **2 Aug 2025** | Rules for **general-purpose AI (GPAI)** models, **governance** bodies, **notified bodies**, **confidentiality**, and most **penalties** apply. |
| **2 Aug 2026** | **The bulk of the Act applies**, including **Annex III high-risk** system obligations and **Article 50 transparency** obligations. *(This is the headline deadline for SMEs.)* |
| **2 Aug 2027** | High-risk obligations for AI that is a safety component of products covered by **Annex I** (existing product-safety law) apply. Extended transition for GPAI models already on the market. |

## How to frame it in the output

- **Article 4 literacy & prohibited practices are already in force.** Flag these as *overdue if not
  yet addressed*, not as future work.
- **2 August 2026** is the date to anchor the urgency: Annex III high-risk + transparency.
- Count down from the current date to 2 Aug 2026 when relevant ("X weeks remaining").
- Annex I product-safety high-risk uses get the longer runway to 2 Aug 2027 — note this if the
  company makes regulated products, otherwise omit.
