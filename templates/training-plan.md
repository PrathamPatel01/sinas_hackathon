## AI-Literacy Plan — [Company]
*Article 4 has been in force since 2 February 2025 — this is overdue, not future work.*

### Baseline module (all staff)
- **Objectives:** understand what AI is in use here, its limits, and basic safe use.
- **Key points:** capabilities & limits (hallucination, bias); data hygiene (what not to paste);
  when to involve a human; how to report a problem.
- **Do/don't:** do verify factual output; don't paste confidential/personal data into public tools.
- **Format:** 30-min onboarding session + 1-page reference.

### Targeted module — HR / recruiting (high-risk exposure)
- **Objectives:** operate the CV-screening tool lawfully as a high-risk deployer.
- **Key points:** bias risk; mandatory human review before any candidate decision; candidate
  transparency; logging duties.
- **Do/don't:** do keep a human in the loop on every shortlist; don't let the tool auto-reject.
- **Format:** 45-min role workshop.
